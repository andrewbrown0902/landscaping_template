Our Story

Family Owned & Locally Operated

Born and raised in the heart of Indianapolis, our family has been tending to the landscapes of this community for over two decades. What started as a single truck and a passion for craftsmanship has grown into the city's premier outdoor living specialist.

We believe that being "locally owned" is more than just a label—it's a commitment. We understand the unique Indiana seasons, the local soil composition, and most importantly, the values of our neighbors in Carmel, Fishers, and beyond.

Common Questions

How much does a typical project cost? expand_more
Project costs vary wildly based on scope. Lawn maintenance plans start at $45/visit, while custom hardscaping projects typically begin at $5,000. We provide firm, detailed quotes after a site visit.
Are you licensed and insured in Indiana? expand_more
Yes, we are fully licensed and maintain $2M in general liability insurance and workers' comp for all employees. Certificates are available upon request.
How long does a hardscape installation take? expand_more
Most patios and walkways are completed within 5-10 business days, depending on complexity and weather. We prioritize finishing one job before moving to the next.
Do you offer a warranty on plants? expand_more
Yes, we offer a 1-year replacement warranty on all trees and shrubs we install, provided an irrigation system is present or a maintenance plan is active.
What happens if it rains on a scheduled day? expand_more
We track weather closely. If rain prevents safe or quality work, we'll notify you via SMS/Email and reschedule for the next available clear day.
